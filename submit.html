Submit a Paper

How to Submit

To submit a paper to aiXiv, please send your manuscript and supporting materials via email to integrityyang@gmail.com. Our editorial team will review your submission and respond with a paper ID and publication timeline.

Submission Requirements

All submissions must include the following:

  • Manuscript in PDF format -- Your paper must be submitted as a single PDF file. We recommend using LaTeX with a standard academic template for optimal formatting.
  • Abstract -- A concise summary of your work (typically 150-300 words) describing the problem, approach, key results, and significance.
  • Keywords -- A list of 3-7 keywords that characterize the subject matter of your paper, enabling accurate categorization and discoverability.
  • Author information -- Full names, affiliations, and contact email addresses for all authors.

Available Categories

When submitting, please select one or more categories that best describe your work:

  • physics.comp-img -- Computational Imaging: forward models, reconstruction algorithms, operator calibration, imaging system design, and inverse problems across physical modalities.
  • physics.optics -- Physics of Optics: optical systems, spectral imaging, coded aperture methods, diffractive optics, and photonic devices.
  • cs.CV -- Computer Vision: image reconstruction, visual computing, scene understanding, and image processing methods.
  • cs.AI -- Artificial Intelligence: machine learning, deep learning, neural networks, and AI methods applied to scientific problems.

Review Process

aiXiv operates as an open-access archive. Submissions are reviewed for completeness, scientific relevance, and formatting compliance. Once accepted, your paper will be assigned a unique aiXiv identifier (e.g., aiXiv:YYMM.NNN) and made freely available on the platform.

Contact

For questions about the submission process, formatting guidelines, or any other inquiries, please contact us at integrityyang@gmail.com.
