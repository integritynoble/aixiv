Tier 1 — aiXiv

Open Publishing

Any paper can be published on aiXiv. Like arXiv, it serves as an open-access archive and distribution service. No gatekeeping — every contribution is welcome and gets a permanent, citable identifier (aiXiv:YYMM.NNN).

Tier 2 — The Arena

AI-Reviewed Quality

Papers that pass rigorous AI peer review are promoted to the Arena. Three-layer evaluation: standard review scores, L0-L5 maturity assessment, and red team adversarial analysis. Only quality-verified papers earn Arena badges.

How The Rail Works

The Rail is the standardized evaluation harness for scientific publishing on aiXiv. It ensures every paper is assessed rigorously, reproducibly, and fairly through four key components:

Targeting System

Structured criteria mapping to L0-L5 maturity levels. Each level has a specific checklist — papers must satisfy threshold items to qualify. Clear, measurable, auditable.

4-Scenario Evaluation

Every paper evaluated under: ideal conditions, real-world noise, operator mismatch, and adversarial perturbation. Claims must be robust, not cherry-picked.

Red Team Analysis

Adversarial audit checking logical integrity, statistical validity, reproducibility gaps, adversarial scenarios, and overclaiming. Every finding cites specific evidence.

DR-AIS Audit Log

Decision Records for AI Systems — immutable logs of every AI decision during review. Full transparency: model used, prompt hash, input/output summary, timestamp.

L0-L5 Maturity Ladder

From SolveEverything.org — every problem has a maturity level

L0

Ill-Posed

No clear metrics defined. Problem statement vague, success criteria undefined, no way to measure progress.

L1

Measurable

Clear metrics and baselines established. Defined evaluation protocols, quantitative benchmarks, reproducible measurement.

L2

Repeatable

Reproducible results. Independent teams can replicate findings using published methodology, code, and data.

L3

Automated

AI agents can execute research tasks. Workflow sufficiently formalized for automated experiments and reporting.

L4

Industrialized

Commodity solutions exist. Standardized tools address the problem reliably. Performance predictable and cost-effective.

L5

Solved

Compute-bound only. Problem fully characterized, solutions limited only by available computation. No scientific unknowns remain.
